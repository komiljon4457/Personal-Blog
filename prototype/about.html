About me

Hi, I’m Anna Weber — a software developer based in Berlin who spends far too much of her free time in the mountains.

I started this blog to keep a record of the trips I take, the things I cook, and the small experiments I run with web technology. It runs on hand-written, semantic HTML and a single stylesheet, and I intend to keep it that way for as long as I can.

What you’ll find here

  • Travel — slow, car-free journeys across Europe.
  • Tech — notes on building simple, durable software.
  • Food — the recipes I actually cook on weeknights.
  • Life — everything that doesn’t fit the boxes above.

Want to say hello or suggest a topic? The contact page is the best way to reach me.
